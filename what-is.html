What is Vishwaroopa Darshanam?

The Vishwaroopa (Universal Form) is the divine, all-encompassing cosmic form of Lord Krishna, revealed to Arjuna in the Bhagavad Gita (Chapter 11) on the battlefield of Kurukshetra. This form transcends human perception, displaying infinite faces, countless arms, blazing eyes, and the entire universe within itself. It symbolizes the infinite power, time, and omnipresence of the divine, illustrating that Krishna is the ultimate reality beyond birth and destruction. Arjuna, overwhelmed by this vision, realizes that Krishna is not just his charioteer but the Supreme Being governing all existence.
