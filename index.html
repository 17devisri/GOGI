Vishwaroopa Darshanam

What is Vishwaroopa Darshanam?

The grand cosmic vision revealed by Lord Krishna to Arjuna, displaying his infinite form.

This divine vision represents the entirety of existence, where Krishna manifests as the universe itself.

The Divine Vision

Krishna appeared with countless faces, arms, and celestial brilliance, beyond time and space.

Arjuna witnessed countless deities, sages, and cosmic wonders within Krishna’s divine form.

Symbolism

Represents the ultimate truth, unity of the universe, and the power of time (Kāla).

This form signifies that all beings exist within Krishna and are bound by the cycle of time.
