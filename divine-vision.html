The Divine Vision

Divine Vision (Divya Drishti) is the spiritual insight granted by the Supreme Being, allowing one to perceive the true, infinite nature of existence beyond the limitations of human senses. In the Bhagavad Gita (Chapter 11, Verse 8), Lord Krishna tells Arjuna that his normal vision cannot behold the Vishwaroopa, and only with Divine Eyes can he witness this cosmic form. This vision represents higher wisdom, spiritual enlightenment, and the ultimate truth that transcends material perception. It signifies that true realization comes not through the eyes, but through divine grace and inner awakening.
